How We Work

Consult

We'll start with a free phone consultation and/or and in-home consultation. You'll share your style with me, your expectations, or ask me anything.

Concept

This step involves initial concept design, costing, and planning. I'll provide you with an idea of overall costs based on how many hours I'll work on your project and estimate a budget for decor and furniture.

Source

Once you approve the concept, I'll start sourcing products for your home. You'll receive detailed descriptions with images, costs, and dimensions of each item. If you're satisfied with my proposal, I'll send you an invoice ans make orders with my suppliers.

Style

Once products are delivered to your home, I'll come and style the house based on our initial plan. You just need to sit back and see the magic happen.
